/r/eveal

Reddit wants users to engage with smaller, less-known communities, and users want to discover new content and communities. Even though Reddit continues to roll out new features to help users discover new subreddits, it's still a difficult task for the user. There are almost 1 million subreddits!

To help users find new communities, I've developed this Chrome extension. Once installed will add a new section under posts on Reddit notifying the user of subreddits that contain content similar to the post they are writing or viewing.
